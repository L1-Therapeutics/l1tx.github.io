Mission

L1 Therapeutics is focused on developing new therapeutic approaches for diseases driven by genomic instability. By applying modern biological insights and technologies to existing therapeutics, we seek to responsibly accelerate meaningful delivery of benefit to patients who need it most at the right time

The company’s initial focus is on oncology, where aberrant activity of mobile genetic elements can drive disease progression and poor long‑term outcomes

Our long‑term vision is to help transform serious diseases into more manageable conditions by slowing the biological processes that drive progression and relapse

Team

L1 Therapeutics was founded by William Brandler, who trained in Natural Sciences at the University of Cambridge and earned a PhD in Human Genomics from the University of Oxford. His background spans cancer genomics and computational biology, with 10+ years of research and industry experience applying large‑scale genomic data to understand disease biology

The company is advised by RORR Consulting, led by Richard Ogden, who is focused on supporting scientific development programs across HIV and oncology
